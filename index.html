About Me

I'm an Electrical Engineering professional specializing in Embedded System Design and PCB Design, with a B.E. from the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), graduating with multiple academic excellence awards.

With 3+ years of hands-on industry experience, I have delivered end-to-end hardware solutions for medical devices (EN 60601-1 certified), industrial automation, automotive surveillance, and IoT platforms. My work spans the full hardware development lifecycle — requirements analysis, schematic design, high-speed PCB layout, signal integrity analysis, firmware architecture, and prototype validation.

Currently working at RISETech on medical device hardware and high-speed System on Module (SoM) design with RK chips. Previously interned at Research Measurements and Instruments (RIMS), specializing in communication protocols and digital measurement systems.
