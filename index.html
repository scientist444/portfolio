About Me

Embedded Electronics Engineer with a proven track record of bridging hardware architecture and low-level software — delivering production-ready systems across medical, aerospace & defense, IoT, and RF domains.

With 2+ years at RISETech (National Science & Technology Park, Islamabad), I have owned the full product lifecycle for 4 commercial embedded products — Ekko medical device (EN 60601-1), Tarantula Mouse, AQI Monitor, and EPIC — from EasyEDA schematic capture and multi-layer PCB layout through JLCPCB manufacturing to STM32 and Renesas MCU firmware delivery. Signal integrity work includes 90Ω controlled impedance USB 2.0 differential pairs and DC power domains from 100 mA to 12 A.

Additional experience in aerospace & defense R&D at RIMS (NASTP Alpha, Rawalpindi) — Test & Measurement Department — where I developed Avionics LRU and Portable Tester ATP tools in Python, NI myRIO/myDAQ LabVIEW data acquisition systems, and an RF spectrum monitoring pipeline on USRP X310 up to 5 GHz / 580 Mbps. Formally recommended by RIMS HR management and RISETech Co-Founder Dr. Muhammad Usman Akram.
